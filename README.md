# 🐉 Dungeon Creatures

A charming multiplayer AI arena-battler and dungeon crawler where you loot, customize, and battle adorable creatures!

## Overview

**Dungeon Creatures** is a cozy roguelike dungeon crawler combined with an asynchronous AI arena battle system. Explore procedurally-designed puzzle dungeons, collect loot and mutations to customize your creatures, then watch them battle other players' creatures in the arena. No PvP stress—just strategic preparation and goofy creature interactions!

**Status:** Pre-Alpha (Core systems in development)

## 🎮 Gameplay Loop

1. **Arena Battle** → Watch your AI-controlled creatures battle in the arena
2. **Dungeon Crawl** → Explore puzzle-based dungeon rooms and collect loot
3. **Customize** → Equip items and apply mutations to power up your creatures
4. **Repeat** → Return to the arena with your enhanced creatures

## ✨ Key Features

- **Cute Art & Goofy Interactions**: Adorable creature designs with hilarious battle animations and behaviors
- **Fast-Paced Dungeon Crawls**: Puzzle-based rooms that demand quick thinking and reflexes
- **Deep Creature Customization**: Mix and match items and mutations to create unique builds
- **AI-Driven Combat**: Let your creatures battle autonomously while you prepare your next run
- **Asynchronous Multiplayer**: Battle other players' creatures without real-time PvP stress

## 🛠️ Requirements

- **Godot Engine:** 4.6+
- **Platform:** Windows, macOS, Linux (native support via Godot)

## 📋 Getting Started

### Installation

1. Clone the repository:
   ```bash
   git clone https://github.com/StrykerZK/dungeon-creatures.git
   ```

2. Open the project in **Godot Engine 4.6**

3. Run the project or export to your platform of choice

### Project Structure

- **Creatures System** - AI behavior, stats, and customization
- **Items System** - Equipment and loot mechanics
- **Mutations System** - Creature augmentations and power-ups
- **Dungeon Generation** - Puzzle room layouts and encounters
- **Arena Battle System** - Combat simulation and results
